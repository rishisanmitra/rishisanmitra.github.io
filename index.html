About Me

I find the answer hiding in the data, and show people what to do about it.

I'm a Data Analyst and BI Developer based in St. Louis, MO, I completed my Master's in Data Analytics at Webster University. Before that, I spent two years working in analytics at Amazon and Medplus Health Services, where I learned something that still shapes how I work: the most important question isn't "what does the data show?" Every project in this portfolio is built around that idea. I work across the full analytics stack, from raw data extraction and SQL modeling through cloud warehousing and BI visualization, because understanding the whole pipeline helps me build better answers, not just better charts.

I'm certified in Microsoft Power BI (PL-300) and experienced across SQL Server, BigQuery, Snowflake, dbt, Python, R, Tableau, and Looker Studio. The tools matter less to me than the question being asked. I pick what fits the problem.

R Statistical Engineering — Device Value Modeling

💡Applied log10 transformation to normalize the heavily right-skewed price distribution into a regression-ready bell curve. Z-scored all hardware specs to enable cross-dimension comparison.
